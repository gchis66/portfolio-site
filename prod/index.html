About Me

Hey, I'm Greg Chisholm. I started out as a front-end developer but lately I've been diving deep into cloud infrastructure and working on making the switch into that space. I like building things that are actually used, whether that's a Chrome extension that picked up hundreds of users or even a palm reading web app that tells fortunes.

I'm big on using AI tools day to day, not just for productivity but for building real projects faster. On the cloud side, I've been learning how to design cost-efficient, scalable setups on AWS, and I'm documenting that journey here.

The mix of front-end, cloud, and AI pretty much sums up where I'm at right now, building, learning, and aiming to bring all of it together as I move into a career in cloud infrastructure.
